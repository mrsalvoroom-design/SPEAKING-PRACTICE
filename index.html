📖 Panduan Pola Jawaban

Cara menjawab berbagai jenis pertanyaan

1

WH Questions

What, When, Where, Who, Why, How

"What time do you wake up?"

→ Subject + usually/often + verb + time
"I usually wake up at 6 AM"

2

Do/Does Questions (Yes/No)

Pertanyaan yang bisa dijawab Ya atau Tidak

"Do you like coffee?"

→ Yes/No + Subject + do/does/don't/doesn't
"Yes, I do. I drink it every morning."

3

Preference (Prefer/Would Rather)

Menyatakan preferensi atau pilihan

"Do you prefer tea or coffee?"

→ I prefer + noun/to verb + rather than
"I prefer tea because it's healthier"

4

Frequency (How often)

Menyatakan seberapa sering melakukan sesuatu

"How often do you exercise?"

→ Subject + frequency adverb + verb
"I usually exercise three times a week"
(always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never)

5

Opinion (What do you think...)

Menyatakan pendapat atau opini

"What do you think about remote work?"

→ In my opinion/I think/From my point of view + reason
"In my opinion, remote work is great because..."

6

Past Experience (Have you ever...)

Pengalaman di masa lalu

"Have you ever been to Japan?"

→ Yes, I have + when / No, I haven't + but...
"Yes, I have. I went there last summer"

7

Future Plan (Would like to/Going to)

Rencana di masa depan

"What would you like to do next year?"

→ I would like to/I'm going to/I plan to + verb
"I would like to learn Spanish next year"

💡 Tips Speaking:

  • • Bicara dengan percaya diri, tidak perlu sempurna
  • • Gunakan filler words: "Well...", "You know...", "Actually..."
  • • Jika lupa kata, gunakan yang sederhana: "thing", "stuff"
  • • Selalu tambahkan WHY/REASON setelah jawaban
  • • Jangan takut berhenti sejenak untuk berpikir
